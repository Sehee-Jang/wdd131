Fitness and Exercise Website Plan

Site Name

The site name for this project is FitLifeHub.

This name was chosen the idea of a central hub for fitness enthusiasts, where they can find exercise routhines, nutritional advice, and community support.

Site Purpose

The purpose of FitLifeHub is to provide a comprehensive resource for individuals who want to improve their health and adopt a healthier lifestyle. The site will offer a variety of content including:

  • Workout routines for various fitness levels
  • Nutrition tips and healthy recipes
  • Motivational articels and success stories
  • A supportive community forum for users to connect and share experiences

Scenarios

Scenario for FitLifeHub

  • What is an effective full-body exercise routine for beginners?
  • Where can I find healthy and delicious recipes to support my health goals?
